Welcome

Welcome to matchaland.net! We are a network of several game servers with a focus on providing players a nice place to chill out, mess around, and have fun.

About us

matchaland was founded in 2023 by bobatealee with the goals of having minimal rules and being advertisement & bloat free. We're big into hosting Team Fortress 2 and TF2 Classic servers, but host for other games as well. While we're just starting out, we plan to stick around for years to come!
